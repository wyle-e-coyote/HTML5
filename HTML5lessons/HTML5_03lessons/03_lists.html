Recipe of the Day:Honeydew Melon

3 cups Honeydew Melon (seeded & chopped) 2 tsp Lime Juice 1 cup Vanilla Nonfat Yogurt 1 cup Ice Cubes

Take a blender, add honeydew melon and watermelon; blend until it smoothens. Mix yogurt, ice and limejuice and blend it again. Transfer it into tall glasses and drink it immediately.

Preparation time 10 Minutes Number of servings (12 oz) 2 Calories per serving 250 295 if 1 tbs sugar added 315 if 1 tbs honey added
